About NIRMAAN

Initiate. Inculcate. Inspire.

19th National Level Technical Symposium

Nirmaan is a National Level Technical Symposium that recognizes the innate capabilities to novice under graduates and make them succeed as future revolutioists of Civil engineering. We aimed at channeling the mountain of potential resources by reinvigorating them through civilized innovaive ideas, dynamic process and sustainable future.

Being concrete about thoughts and reinforcing the objectives in this year 2020, we proudly present Nirmaan & this would definitely make you experience the newest trend of civil engineering.

We, the team NIRMAAN, by your support would be pleased to present the worlds master minds of Civil Engineering.

About Us

The Department of Civil Engineering. UCE(A). Osmania University was established in the year 1929, with a vision to be as a leading academic department on pace with global standards and contribute to the regional growth and meet the challenges of Civil Engineering Profession.
